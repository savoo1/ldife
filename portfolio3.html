Sonoma Residence

back to portfolio +

This home was designed in collaboration with McMahon Architects & Studio in San Francisco. This home is designed to reflect a classical layout with contemporary design details. The open space plan serves to encompass the outside views into it's interior.

The home was kept clean and unfussy. In this home the motto â€œless is more" perfectly describes to absence of heavy molding that you would traditionally see in a traditional floor plan. The living has a dramatic antique style fireplace surround juxtaposed against the clean lines of the room and the concrete floors.

Open to the dining and living room the kitchen highlights the copper hood by keeping the wall open and tiling the entire wall. All of the cabinetry and storage are set on the rooms' sides and also in the storage rooms behind the stove

A massive walk-in shower sits at the end of a light filled master bath. The clean line concept is kept consistent throughout even in the fixtures selected like this freestanding tub.
