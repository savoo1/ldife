Villa Montana Club-House

back to portfolio +

The club-house needed to be transitional enough to satisfy the many tastes of various homeowners in the community.

The furnishings needed to handle large groups of people for gatherings, Fabrics and colors that could handle stains a were used. The room needed to feel like a comfortable living room, lot's of seating and table options were placed through-out.

A romantic kitchen area with a wood beaded chandelier hangs near a black and white photo of Sophia Lauren. The nook serves as a smaller meeting area for guests.

Another seating area looks out onto the preserve.

The x-benches rest under a limestone and bronze console table. The benches proved useful for moving around during larger gathering events.

The pedestal adds a hint of upscale contemporary elegance to the space.
