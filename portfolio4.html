This gorgeous Santa Barbara style home with contemporary flair is built in Austin Texas. This home looks out to a wilderness preserve and has abundant light streaming in from the windows.

A contemporary take on Moroccan from Exquisite Surfaces sets the mood for this amply spaced kitchen. Neolith and Silestone are used for the counter surfaces.

Walnut floors and wood beams add warmth to the kitchen area.

Continuing with Moroccan theme hand cut tiles are imported for the powder room. A transparent chocolate glass circular sink sits above a Silestone countertop.
